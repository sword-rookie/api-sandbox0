Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I exceed my sandbox limit?

On the Free tier, you'll need to wait for your monthly cycle to reset or upgrade. On Pro, we offer soft-limits with metered overage charges of $0.50 per additional sandbox.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes. You can cancel through your dashboard instantly. You'll maintain Pro access until the end of your current billing period.

Do you offer educational discounts?

We provide 50% off Pro plans for verified students and researchers. Contact our support team with your .edu email to apply.
